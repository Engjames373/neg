NEG Irrigation Controller

At the heart of our product lineup is the NEG Irrigation Controller, a cutting-edge device that revolutionizes water management for farmers across Tanzania. This state-of-the-art innovation leverages advanced sensor technology and sophisticated algorithms to precisely monitor soil moisture levels and automatically adjust irrigation schedules, empowering farmers to optimize water usage and maximize crop yields.

NEG Soil Moisture Sensor

Developed through extensive research and field testing, the NEG Soil Moisture Sensor is a game-changer in the world of precision farming. Unlike traditional handheld meters, our sensor is installed directly in the soil, providing a constant stream of accurate, reliable data that can be accessed and analyzed through a user-friendly mobile app or web platform. This allows farmers to track soil moisture levels across their entire property, identifying hotspots, optimizing irrigation schedules, and ensuring that their crops are receiving the perfect amount of water at all times.

NEG Soil Moisture Meter

The NEG Soil Moisture Meter is a state-of-the-art device that provides farmers with highly accurate and reliable measurements of soil moisture levels. Featuring advanced sensor technology, the meter can precisely detect the amount of water content in the soil, empowering farmers to make informed decisions about irrigation, fertilization, and crop management. Unlike traditional handheld meters that only provide spot-checks, the NEG Soil Moisture Meter offers a comprehensive, real-time view of soil moisture conditions across an entire field or plot of land.

Irrigation Systems and farm structures Services

At NEG-AGRITECHNOLOGIES, we understand that a well-designed irrigation system is the foundation for successful and efficient agricultural operations. Our team of experienced engineers and agronomists works closely with farmers to assess their unique land conditions, crop requirements, and water resources, developing custom-tailored irrigation system designs that maximize productivity while minimizing waste and environmental impact.

Farm Management Services

At NEG-AGRITECHNOLOGIES, we understand that effective farm management goes beyond just the tools and technologies we provide. That's why we offer a suite of comprehensive farm management services designed to give our clients a complete, data-driven overview of their agricultural operations. Through detailed topographic surveys, aerial imagery, and advanced soil analysis, we create detailed maps of our clients' farms, pinpointing areas of optimum productivity, identifying problem zones, and tracking changes in soil composition and water availability over time. This invaluable data allows farmers to make informed decisions about irrigation, crop rotation, and resource allocation, ultimately driving sustainable growth and profitability.

Extension Services

To ensure that our clients fully leverage the capabilities of our innovative technologies and solutions, we provide comprehensive training programs covering everything from proper installation and maintenance to advanced data analysis and decision-making. Through hands-on workshops, interactive tutorials, and ongoing support, we empower farmers to become experts in the use of our products, enabling them to extract maximum value and achieve their agricultural goals with confidence.
